Tornado history:

2021 featured the first recorded tornado of EF3 force. A tornado of EF3 has estimated winds of 158-206mph. An EF3 is also characterized by severe damage. According to the Enhanced Fujita Scale(EF Scale), Roofs and some walls torn from well-constructed houses; trains overturned; most trees in forested area uprooted; heavy cars lifted and thrown.

This information was taken from the National Weather Service.

Further information on the Enhanced Fujita Scale can be found by following this link: https://www.weather.gov/oun/efscale
